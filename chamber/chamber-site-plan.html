Bellville Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Bellville Chamber of Commerce

This name represents Bellville, Texas, where the chamber operates, aiming to connect local businesses and promote economic growth.

Site Purpose

The Bellville Chamber of Commerce website will:

  • Provide a platform for local businesses to network and collaborate.
  • Offer resources and information to support economic development and community engagement.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

The color schema includes the following colors:

Primary Color (for headings and key elements)
Secondary Color (for backgrounds and accents)
Accent Color (for highlights and call-to-action buttons)

Typography

The typography for the site includes the following fonts:

  • El Messiri: Used for body text for readability and a clean look.
  • Ubuntu Sans: Used for headings and important text to make them stand out.
